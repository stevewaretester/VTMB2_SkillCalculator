Codex page title: "Glide"

After beginning a Jump, hold the Jump button for longer to initiate a Glide. While Gliding, it is possible to change course by pressing a direction.

Related codex tip:

Whilst airborne, press [Dash] to get a burst of speed forward. Useful for jumping between rooftops.

Codex body Press [TK] to pick up object. Press [TK] again to drop it. Press [Throw] to throw held objects.
Codex tip Pickup/Pull weapons from enemies using Telekinesis, then throw them at a target.
Codex tip Important TK-grabbable objects are highlighted purple when using Heightened Senses.
Codex tip The Nomad can use a Telekinetic Pull on an unaware opponent — brings them within striking distance or opens them to stronger assaults.
Weapons page Ranged weapons with ammo can be fired back at assailants. Once ammo is empty, they can be Telekinetically thrown.

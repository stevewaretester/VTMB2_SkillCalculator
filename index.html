How to unlock abilities ▶

In-Clan — Unlock abilities in sequence (Passive → Strike → Relocate → Affect → Mastery → Perk) by spending AP.

Cross-Clan — To unlock another clan's ability:
① Awaken it at the Clan Trainer by spending Blood Resonance.
② Unlock the equivalent ability in your own clan tree first.
③ Spend AP — usually more than in-clan cost, but discounted 50% if your clan shares affinity.

Cross-Clan Perks — To unlock another clan's Perk:
① Unlock your own clan's Perk first.
② Awaken every ability in the target clan's tree.
③ Spend 6 AP to unlock their Perk.

Partial Resonance Reactions

Some NPCs will be Partially Resonant, meaning you can cause them to become fully resonant, but this requires two things.
① An outfit they will be willing to talk to you wearing.
② The correct dialogue choice.
The below catalogues the responses you will get, depending on your choices of outfit and speech.

Make a Haven a Home

A conversion mod for Phyre’s Haven, activated by collecting the Dead Cat codex, and applied any time entering/exiting the Haven.

In addition to cleaning up the place you get the following features:

Screenshot placeholder

Blood Alchemy

Located in the kitchen.

Phyre has found some of Dale’s recipes and is capable of creating not just thin-blood elixirs, but also exchanging resonances for each other, Ability Points, max Elixir count, and even max HP.

Screenshot placeholder

Shadow Broker

Requires Clan Completion Talents Lasombra Clan Completion

Located in the bathroom area, the Shadow Broker is a mysterious entity that can provide an assortment of deadly weapons, stored directly in your shadowcache, all at the cost of Melancholic resonance.

Screenshot placeholder

Blood Bath

Phyre finally gets to wash, but in blood. Located beside the Shadow Broker, you can take a dip in the tub to restore all lost HP and Blood pips.

Fast Travel

Requires Prince permission to move about the town

Once you’re able to move freely, Phyre can use Dale’s notes and maps to travel through backdoors to each Elysium without being noticed or back again, indicated by the neon bat sign.

Window Escape

As a creature of the night, making use of a window to enter and exit the Haven makes sense, and now you can achieve exactly that.

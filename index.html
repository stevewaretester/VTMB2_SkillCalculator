How to unlock abilities ▶

In-Clan — Unlock abilities in sequence (Passive → Strike → Relocate → Affect → Mastery → Perk) by spending AP.

Cross-Clan — To unlock another clan's ability:
① Unlock the equivalent ability in your own clan tree first.
② Awaken it at the Clan Trainer by spending Blood Resonance.
③ Spend AP — usually more than in-clan cost, but discounted 50% if your clan shares affinity.
Note: this calculator ignores the equivalent-unlock requirement.

Cross-Clan Perks — To unlock another clan's Perk:
① Unlock your own clan's Perk first.
② Awaken every ability in the target clan's tree.
③ Spend 6 AP to unlock their Perk.

Partial Resonance Reactions

Some NPCs will be Partially Resonant, meaning you can cause them to become fully resonant, but this requires two things.
① An outfit they will be willing to talk to you wearing.
② The correct dialogue choice.
